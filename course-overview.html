Chapter 3

Why may a person need assistance?

Understanding why a person may need assistance with medication is crucial for providing appropriate and dignified support. There are various reasons why service users might require help with their medication, and recognizing these needs is a key skill for care assistants.

Common Reasons for Medication Assistance

Physical Limitations

Many service users experience physical difficulties that make self-administering medication challenging.

Cognitive Factors

Cognitive conditions can significantly impact a person's ability to manage their own medication.

Environmental Factors

Sometimes environment creates barriers to proper medication management.
